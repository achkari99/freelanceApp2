## Step 1: Name the moment

Contextualize the shift your customers are navigating. Resist the urge to lead with features.

## Step 2: Paint the journey

Describe the old way versus the new. Use narrative arcs, not product screenshots, to show contrast.

## Step 3: Offer a proof loop

Anchor claims with evidence. Combine hero metrics, customer quotes, and usage moments to build trust.
